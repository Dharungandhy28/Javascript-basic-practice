Basic Loops in js

There are 4 types of loops they are:

1.  For loop:

syntax:
for(initialization; condition; increament/decreament){
codes
}

2.  while loop:

syntax:
while (condition)  
{  
 codes
}

3.  Do while loop:

syntax:
do{  
 codes  
}while (condition);

4.  for in loop:
    For in loops are used to iterated the objects and examples are in day 5

syntax:
for(var key in objects){
codes:
